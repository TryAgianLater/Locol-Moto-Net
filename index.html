How it works: Riders submit trail runs with GPS logs or video evidence. Our community reviews submissions and assigns rankings based on performance metrics. Rankings and licenses are updated monthly.

📊 System Overview

License Levels

🏆 Pro

Elite riders with consistent top performance. Minimum 3+ years competitive trail riding experience.

⭐ Pro-Am

Advanced intermediate riders pushing for pro status. Demonstrated skill across multiple trail types.

📈 Intermediate

Solid riders with good fundamentals. Consistent performance on technical sections.

🎯 Amateur

Developing riders. Entry level with basic trail skills and growing experience.

Rating Types

🎯 Overall Rating

Composite score (0–100) combining all performance metrics. Your headline ranking.

⚡ Single Lap Pace

Peak speed and precision on one clean run. Best lap time relative to class average.

🔄 Enduro Pace

Consistency over multiple laps. How well you maintain performance as fatigue sets in.

🎪 Moto Consistency

Reliability across different trail conditions and rider rotation events.

💪 Adaptability

BOOST ONLY: Advanced technique bonus. Never lowers score. Rewards riders who excel in varied terrain.
